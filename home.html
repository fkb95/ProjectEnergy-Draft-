What's Project Energy?

P.E. is a web application for electronic devices and their technical specifications.
This website allows you to find the device you're searching for and read about its consumptions.
If your intention is to buy an electrodomestic or a new smartphone, maybe you will find something usefull here.
"The less its consumption, the higher is your saving income."

No account needed.
ProjectEnergy is done for everyone.

Our goal

Pretty simple: If you buy low consumptions products, your bills will be less expensive.
If companies see that people want products with low consumptions, they will make them.
If houses need less energy, the entire world will work better.

This is cool, isn't it?
